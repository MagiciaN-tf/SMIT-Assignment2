The Adventure in the Forest

Chapter 1

John had been gazing out of his cabin window for about an hour now. On his table, amidst the maps, compasses, and survival guides, was a well-worn copy of The Lost Expedition. It had been John's favorite book since his days in the military, and the longer he spent in the dense forest, the more he felt the need to explore.

He had spent the last ten years in this wilderness, sitting under a banner with a quote from an ancient explorer that declared Adventure is worthwhile in itself. Despite many recognizing his feats in discovering rare species and mapping uncharted territories, John did believe he had more exciting adventures to embark upon.
